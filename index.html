New York MTA Monthly Ridership

Brodie Henson

The Metropolitan Transportation Authority, or MTA, is the regional public transportation agency of New York. The organization includes many services, such as the subway and the New York City Transit, or NYCT, bus. In 2023, the MTA recorded over 1.15 billion rides on the subway and 340 million rides on the NYCT bus (Subway and Metro-North Railroad) Many New York residents use public transit multiple times a day, whether to commute, go to an event or simply to travel to another neighborhood.

Source: Data.gov, MTA Monthly Ridership / Traffic Data: Beginning January 2008.

The chart above shows the monthly ridership for the subway, NYCT bus and the Long Island Rail Road, or LIRR. Subway ridership is, by far, the highest among MTA services offered; it is the most used form of public transportation in New York.
